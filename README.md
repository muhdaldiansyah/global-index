## Sources

### PISA 2022
* OECD – *PISA 2022 Results (Volume I)*
* OECD Education GPS – country profiles & data tables
* FactsMaps – compiled average scores (Math, Reading, Science)
* NCES (U.S.) – *Fast Facts* (konfirmasi partisipasi negara)
* Catatan: Luxembourg & India tidak ikut PISA 2022; data provinsi Tiongkok tidak dipublikasikan

### HDI 2023
* UNDP – *Human Development Report 2025, Statistical Annex* (Table 1: HDI 2023)
* UNDP – Data Center & country insights (metodologi & tren HDI)

### HAQ Index 2019
* Lancet Global Health – *Healthcare access and quality index based on mortality from causes amenable to personal health care in 195 countries and territories* (GBD 2019)
* IHME – *Global Health Data Exchange (GHDx)* (dataset HAQ Index per negara)
* Catatan: HAQ Index 2019 adalah rilis terbaru; skala 0-100 (overall, usia 0-74)

### GDP 2023 (Nominal, US$ Trillion)
* IMF – *World Economic Outlook (WEO) Database* (NGDPD, current US$)
* UN Statistics – *National Accounts Main Aggregates*
* World Bank – *World Development Indicators (WDI), GDP (current US$)*
* Taiwan (Chinese Taipei) – DGBAS (estimasi resmi 2023: ~US$755–757 miliar)
* Hong Kong C&SD, Macao DSEC – GDP official statistics

**Metodologi Komposit:** HDI 2023 + GDP nominal 2023 + PISA 2022 dinormalisasi 0-1, lalu dirata-rata.
